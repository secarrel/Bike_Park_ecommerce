Requirements

To ride at the park you must have the following equipment as a minimum:
  • A helmet with MIPS. If you are riding any black trails you should have a full face helmet.
  • Body armour with a suitable amount of protection for the trails you are riding and also your experience/confidence level.
  • A maintained and fully functioning mountain bike that is suitable for the trails you will be riding.
  • Suitable shoes and clothing for the conditions.
  • Spare parts and tools to allow you to be self-sufficient on the trails.
