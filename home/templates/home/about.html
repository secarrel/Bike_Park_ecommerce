About FastrTrack Bike Park

Here you'll find everything you need to know to ensure you have a smooth and stress fre visit to our park, including: opening hours, uplift times, our location and directions!

Find Us

We are located near Talybont-on-Usk with a huge offering of local campsites and amenities. Located on the side of the mountain, we have incredible views of Llangorse reservoir and the beautiful surround mountainous landscape.

It's easiest to find us by following the A40 and joining Station Road.

The park is well sign posted, just keep going until you find the carpark!

All riders must sign in before taking to the trails. Reception opens at the times specified above but all riders must be off the trails 30 minutes before park closes. All other facilities will be available until the specified closing time.

The uplift will be running from 30 minutes after opening time to an hour before closing time.
